Contact and Note manager

  • A contact manager is a web app that enables users to easily store contact information such as name, address, telephone number and email address. you can add up to five contact information and have them all saved up.

  • Note manager helps user store information about a particular entity. It could be a memo, To-do, or even a personal noe to self. You can add as many notes as possible and has saved

How to add contact

5 easy steps

  1. Click on add contact
  2. Fill in the required informaion
  3. Click on 'add' (a notification would immediateky pop up on your screen 'Added successfully')
  4. Add as many contact as possible and saved them
  5. Click on the next page to view your saved contacts

How to add notes

  1. Click on add notes
  2. Text area for writting notes and memos
  3. Click on 'add' (a notification would immediately pop up on your screen 'Added successfully')
  4. Add as many notes as you want
  5. Click on the next page to view saved notes
